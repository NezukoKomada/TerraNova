Problem Overview

  • Climate change: Waste in landfills releases greenhouse gases.
  • High costs: Waste management is expensive for small communities.
  • Inequitable impact: Low-income areas face health and property issues.
  • Health risks: Poor waste handling causes skin and respiratory issues.
  • Safety hazards: Pathogens, chemicals, and fires at waste sites.
  • Worker safety: Risk of injuries and exposure to harmful materials.
